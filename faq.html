Frequently Asked Questions

Who can register on Agrofy?
Farmers, buyers, and transport providers can register on Agrofy to connect and grow their business.
How do I upload my products?
After login, verified users can go to the upload page, fill details, and submit for admin verification.
Is Agrofy free to use?
Yes, Agrofy is completely free for all users to register, connect, and explore services.
How can I contact support?
You can visit our Contact page or send an email through the contact form available there.
